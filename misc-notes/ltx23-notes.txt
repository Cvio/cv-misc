### Quick Usage Tips for Wan 2.2

* **High vs. Low Noise:** Keep in mind that Wan 2.2 uses a Mixture-of-Experts (MoE) architecture. Many Wan 2.2 LoRAs come with both a "High Noise" model (which dictates the overall structure and layout) and a "Low Noise" model (which handles fine textures and micro-movements). You will often need to load both and balance their weights depending on the desired effect.
* **Step Counts:** If you are using optimization LoRAs like the *Wan2.2-Lightning* distilled models, remember to drastically drop your inference steps (usually down to 4–8) and lower your guidance scale to avoid deep-frying your generations. 
* **Trigger Words:** Even if a LoRA lists a specific trigger word, Wan 2.2's prompt adherence is remarkably strong. You can often get away with natural language descriptions instead of rigidly spamming the trigger token.
