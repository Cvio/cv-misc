Unlike Seedance, Kling offers several different versions tailored to specific needs and budgets:

* **Kling 3.0 / V3 Omni:** The newest flagship model. It features "Omni Native Audio" (perfect lip-syncing, dialogue, and sound effects generated simultaneously with the video), multi-shot storyboarding, and up to 15 seconds of continuous 4K/60fps video.
* **Kling 2.6 & V2.5 Turbo:** The reliable, production-ready workhorses. They support highly accurate text-to-video and smooth camera motions, acting as more budget-friendly alternatives to the V3 models.
* **Kling Video O1 / O3:** Specialized models focusing on precise video-to-video editing, layout controls, and scene refinement.

Because Kling AI is officially launched globally, you do not need to use workarounds, VPNs, or virtual phone numbers to access it. You have two main paths:
